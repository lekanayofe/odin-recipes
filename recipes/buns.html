Description

Nigerian buns are popular Nigerian street foods. They have sweet and crunchy exterior and soft pillow interior.

Ingridients

  • All Purpose Flower
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Flavouring of choice
  • Oil for deep frying

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingridients together
  2. Create well in middle of dry ingredients bowl and pour wet ingridients
  3. Mix until it forms a thick batter
  4. Allow batter to rest for 5 minutes
  5. Heat up oil in the meantime
  6. Use hand to scoop batter into ball shape
  7. Drop scooped up batter into oil
  8. Fry unti golden brown
  9. Repeat steps 6 and 8 until batter is exhausted
  10. Eat your homemade Nigerian Buns
