Description

The Mai Shayi Noodles is common with Northern street vendors. Usually made with other spices like suya and Yaji. This specific Mai Shayi Noodles is what I make at home for Suhoor during this current Ramadan. Within 10 minutes it's ready for consumption.

Ingridients

  • Instant Noodles
  • Eggs
  • Onions
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Cook instant noodles in a pot with appropriate water
  2. Break Egg into bowl and cut dice onions into egg
  3. Once Instant noodles is done, transfer from pot into egg bowl
  4. Mix instant noodles with egg
  5. Fry instant noodles egg mixture
  6. Enjoy makeshift Mai Shayi Noodles
